Speaker Info

Next to the work I do, speaking is something that I really love. I've been speaking for a little while now, and I welcome any opportunity get up in front of folks to talk about technology in the space I work in.

If you'd like me to speak at a conference you're organizing, let me just say that I'm honored that you'd even consider me in the first place. There's just a couple of things we need to talk about.

Do you have a code of conduct?

This is key. I want to ensure that the event you're organizing has a comprehensive code of conduct that sets the ground rules for everyone involved. Your event should be a place where anyone and everyone can feel like they belong, and that they're safe to be the person that they are.

Do you arrange travel and lodging?

Air travel and lodging is not a trivial cost for most people. Unless your event is in or near the Twin Cities where I live, I'll need to know if you cover these costs for your speakers.

Topics I cover

I talk about a lot of stuff, but below is a list of stuff I've talked/can talk about at your event:

  • Web performance (my strong suit!)
  • HTML, CSS or JavaScript stuff.
  • Whatever ideas you might have!
